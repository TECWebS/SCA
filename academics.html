Academics at Sinai Christian Academy

Our Curriculum

Built on the ABeka Books Foundation

Sinai Christian Academy uses the ABeka Books curriculum — one of the nation's most respected Christian educational programs. ABeka provides a comprehensive, phonics-based, and biblically integrated approach to learning that has been trusted by Christian schools for over 60 years.

From the very first days of Pre-K through the rigors of 12th Grade, our students follow a structured academic path designed to challenge them, ground them in faith, and prepare them for every opportunity ahead.

  • ✓ Phonics-first reading and language arts from the earliest grades
  • ✓ Structured math sequences that build mastery year over year
  • ✓ Biblically integrated history, science, and social studies
  • ✓ College-preparatory rigor beginning in middle school

Personalized Practice

IXL Learning

IXL provides personalized, adaptive practice in math, language arts, science, and social studies. Students work at their own pace while the platform identifies gaps and provides targeted exercises to build mastery.

Online Curriculum Platform

Ignitia is a faith-based online curriculum platform offering interactive, self-paced courses in core subjects. Aligned with ABeka's biblical worldview, it brings 21st-century digital learning to SCA's classrooms.

Every Stage of Learning

From First Steps to Graduation

Our Pre-K through 12th Grade program is carefully designed to meet students where they are and take them where they're going.

Early Childhood Pre-K – K

Early Childhood Program

Nurturing curiosity, building foundational literacy and numeracy, and instilling a love of learning from the very beginning — in a safe, faith-centered environment.

Elementary 1st – 5th

Elementary School

Core academics deepen with structured ABeka instruction. Students build reading fluency, mathematical reasoning, and character through faith-integrated learning.

Middle School 6th – 8th

Middle School

A critical transition period. Students tackle increasingly complex coursework, explore electives, and begin developing the independence and leadership they'll carry into high school.

High School 9th – 12th

High School

College-preparatory rigor, expanded course catalog, athletics, and a senior experience that equips graduates for higher education and Kingdom-minded service.

Our Commitment

Education That Shapes the Whole Person

At Sinai Christian Academy, we believe academic excellence and spiritual formation are not in competition — they are partners. Our classrooms are places where students learn to think critically, grow in faith, and develop the character to serve their communities with integrity.

From ABeka's structured curriculum to our modern technology platforms, every resource we invest in exists to help each student reach their full God-given potential.
