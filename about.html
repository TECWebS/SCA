Who We Are

About Sinai Christian Academy

A Christ-centered community in Linden, NJ, committed to nurturing students academically, spiritually, and personally — from Pre-K all the way through graduation.

Our History

A Legacy of Faith & Learning

Sinai Christian Academy was founded with a singular conviction: that every child deserves an education rooted in faith, excellence, and community. For over 50 years, our school has stood in Linden, NJ as a beacon for families seeking a Christian alternative to public education.

From our early beginnings to our current campus on Grier Avenue, SCA has grown into a Pre-K through 12th Grade institution that graduates students who are prepared not only for college, but for life — anchored in their faith and ready to lead.

We are a proud member of the Association of Christian Schools International (ACSI) and hold accreditation that reflects our commitment to academic rigor and institutional excellence.

Mission & Vision

Who We're Called to Be

Our mission is to provide a quality Christian education that develops the whole child — mind, body, and spirit — in a safe, nurturing environment that honors God and equips students for a purpose-driven life.

We believe that when faith and learning walk together, students don't just earn a diploma — they discover their calling.

What Drives Us

Our Core Values

✝️

Christ-Centered

Faith is not an add-on — it is the foundation. Every classroom, every lesson, and every relationship at SCA is grounded in biblical truth.
